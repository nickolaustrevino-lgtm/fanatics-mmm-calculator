How to Use This Calculator

Purpose: This tool helps Fanatics Sportsbook & Casino paid media teams estimate incremental ROAS and contribution across key channels using Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM) concepts. Use it for quarterly planning, budget allocation decisions, and scenario testing.

Instructions:

  • Enter Weekly Spend: Input your projected or actual weekly media spend for each channel in USD.
  • Enter CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost): Your average cost to acquire one first-time depositor (FTD) for that channel.
  • Enter Average LTV (Lifetime Value): The predicted 365-day LTV for customers acquired through this channel based on our analytics models.
  • Click Calculate: The tool applies channel-specific adstock, saturation, and incrementality adjustments to estimate true incremental impact.
  • Review Recommendations: Optimization insights appear below results to guide budget reallocation conversations with Commercial, Finance, and PMG.

Context for Fanatics: We’re running roughly a 45% brand / 55% lower-funnel mix on a ~$300M annual budget across Sportsbook and Casino. This calculator reflects our philosophy that last‑click platform data over‑credits retargeting and brand search, while MMM highlights true incremental contribution. Use these outputs as signal—not gospel—for scenario planning with Jamie’s team.

Meta, TikTok, Snapchat prospecting. Critical for awareness in newer states where Fanatics is still building sports betting consideration.

YTYouTube / Video

Pre-roll, bumper, and TrueView campaigns. Strong upper‑funnel driver with 14–21 day adstock; key around tentpoles (NFL, March Madness).

CTConnected TV

Streaming partners (Hulu, Roku, etc.). Premium targeting, strong brand lift. 21–35 day impact window for live sports inventory.

RTRetargeting (All Platforms)

Display, social, and search retargeting of site visitors and app openers. Captures recycled demand; platform ROAS looks great, incrementality does not.

AFAffiliate / Influencer

Performance partnerships, betting influencers, and affiliate networks. Watch for attribution overlap; strong when paired with FanCash and offer strategy.
